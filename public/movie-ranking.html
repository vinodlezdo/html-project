The Best Movies 2023 According to Vinod

My top 3 movie list 2023!

  1. Por Thozhil

    Por Thozhil is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language crime thriller film co-written and directed by Vignesh Raja in his directorial debut.

  2. Good night

    3. Good Night is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Vinayak Chandrasekaran in his directorial debut.

  3. Theera Kadhal

    In a relationship during college, Gowtham and Aaranya marry different people. They accidentally meet on a train to Mangalore, but Gowtham returns to life with his family, while Aaranya remains stuck in a spiderweb of her husband's violence. When Aaranya dares to leave, she tries to pursue Gowtham, creating turmoil and hurt.
